About the Project

Smart Diagnostics for Aquaculture

AquaDiag uses an advanced deep learning pipeline with feature fusion and a custom classification head, where multiple models extract complementary features that are combined before final prediction for higher accuracy. Designed for fish farmers, aquaculture researchers, and veterinary professionals, it delivers fast and reliable diagnosis without expensive lab testing.

Users can upload images, receive instant diagnoses, and submit feedback to continuously improve model accuracy — creating a self-improving diagnostic ecosystem.

Disease Detection

7 Conditions Identified

Our model is trained to recognize the most common and economically significant fish diseases, including healthy baseline classification.

🔴 Bacterial
Bacterial Red Disease

Causes hemorrhagic lesions and reddening of skin and fins. Highly contagious in pond environments with poor water quality.

🦠 Bacterial
Aeromoniasis

Caused by Aeromonas bacteria — presents as ulcers, dropsy, and internal hemorrhaging. Common in freshwater species.

🫁 Bacterial
Bacterial Gill Disease

Infects gill tissue causing respiratory distress and reduced oxygen uptake. Often triggered by crowding and stress.

🍄 Fungal
Saprolegniasis

Fungal infection appearing as white cotton-like patches on skin or fins. Often secondary to wounds or bacterial infection.

🐛 Parasitic
Parasitic Diseases

Includes ectoparasites and endoparasites causing lesions, fin damage, and behavioral changes. Detected by visual skin patterns.

⚡ Viral
White Tail Disease

Viral disease causing white discoloration of the tail region. Spreads rapidly in dense aquaculture settings.

✅ Healthy
Healthy Fish

Baseline classification for normal, disease-free fish — confirming good health and helping track improvement post-treatment.

Platform Features

Built for Real-World Use

🔀
Feature Fusion Architecture

Multiple custom finetuned CNN models run in parallel, with their feature maps concatenated before the final classifier to combine spatial and channel features, improving accuracy beyond single models.

🤖
Ensemble Deep Learning

Powered by multiple custom CNN models and fused feature maps — combining the strengths of two state-of-the-art architectures for higher accuracy.

📊
Confidence Scoring

Every prediction includes a percentage confidence score so you can gauge reliability and make informed decisions.

🕘
Prediction History

Your full diagnosis history is saved — review past predictions, track patterns, and monitor fish health over time.

💬
User Feedback System

Spot a wrong prediction? Submit a correction. Feedback is reviewed by admins and used to improve future model training.

🔐
Secure Authentication

Password-hashed user accounts with role-based access. Separate admin login for dataset and feedback management.
